The Glossary

authenticate
To verify someone is who they claim to be.
authorize
To verify someone is allowed to go where they want to go.

About

The Glossary is a dictionary of technical terms with plain definitions. It was started in frustration when I couldn't keep the difference between authorization and authentication straight in my head. As a result, they are the first two terms with a definition.

To contribute, either by adding a definition, requesting a definition, or suggesting an alternate definition, make an issue or pull-request for this page's GitHub Repo. If that seems daunting to you, send an email to steven@nuclearsandwich.com.
